<h2>Remnants of Shock</h2>  
<!-- /wp:heading -->  
  
<!-- wp:paragraph -->  
<p>Withered branches, bony fingers,<br />scrape a sea of forever cloud.<br />Ash falls in soft snow clumps, <br />embracing land with quiet tenderness. </p>  
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->  
  
<!-- wp:paragraph -->
