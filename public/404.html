404- Page Not Found

Buttons were pressed out of sequence.
Incorrect commands inputted by careless
fingers, not paying enough attention.
Backups all failed together, naive workers
clicked a suspicious email link. Every
system fails eventually. No morning light
wakes us now, no stars or moon watching.
The sun has failed to load, the sky
is replaced with letters, spelling out
a scrambled, mile high error message.
