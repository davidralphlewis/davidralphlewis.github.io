Hello Spring

Published on: July 29, 2024

#posts #photos


spring is like a perhaps
Hand in a window
(carefully to
and fro moving New and
Old things,while
people stare carefully
moving a perhaps
fraction of flower here placing
an inch of air there)and
without breaking anything.

  • E.E. Cummings>
