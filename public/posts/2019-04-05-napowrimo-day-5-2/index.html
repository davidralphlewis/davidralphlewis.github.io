NaPoWriMo 2019 Day 5

Published on: July 29, 2024

#posts #poetry

Dissonance

Under a static sky, we speak
in dial-up modem screeches.
Number stations replace news.
Books become junk code. Still, some
claim to see patterns in the flux.
