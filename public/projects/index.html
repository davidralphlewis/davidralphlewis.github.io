Projects

Larger things I have out in the world. Most of the books are available for free download. No email address or anything required, just click through

Short Stories

  • Amber Stars: One Night of Stories is the first book of short stories I wrote, all set in one night in a small town. Free download.

Poetry

  • Our Voice in the Chaos was my first pamphlet originally published by Selcouth Station, mixing blackout poetry and normal poetry to show the collapse of society. Free download.
  • Spare Parts was a collaboration with M. Amelia Eilki. She wrote flash fiction and I created blackout poetry from it.
  • Refraction is a micro pamphlet about swimming. Free download.
  • Reality Adjustments A small interactive poetry pamphlet written in TWINE, about a city that is always changing. Free to play here.
  • Emergence- A poetry pamphlet based around the idea of panpsychicism, or consciousness as an emergent property of the universe. An excuse to mess around with form. Still got a few left, link takes you to the shop.

Mini Zines

I've made a few little zines as a way of making something from poems.

  • How not to make things perfectly - (Direct pdf download) - A very quick zine about making things quickly. More details and the text of the zine here.
  • G Man (Direct pdf download) - Weird poem about an ancient pagan deity being tortured by a shadowy government agency. I like performing this one.
  • Poetry Dice Zine (Direct pdf download) - Zine about making poems from dice rolls as a quick warm up exercise. Post is here
  • Quota (Direct pdf download) - Another weird poem, this time about climate change. Made with rubber stamps and red ink.
  • Serenity Study- Old zine I made during the pandemic. About things that bring me peace.
